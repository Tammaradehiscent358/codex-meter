# Community standards

Keep discussions useful, respectful, and focused on improving the product.

- Be direct without being dismissive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Make space for contributors with different experience levels.
- Do not post private data, credentials, harassment, or spam.

Maintainers may edit or remove content that breaks these standards and may restrict participation when necessary. Serious conduct concerns can be reported privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile.
